Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our organization and how you can help.

How can I make a donation?

You can make a donation through our secure online portal, via bank transfer, or by mailing a check to our headquarters. All donations are tax-deductible.

What types of volunteer opportunities are available?

We offer both local and international volunteer opportunities in areas like education, healthcare, community development, and administrative support.

How are my donations used?

85% of all donations go directly to our programs, with only 15% allocated to administrative and fundraising costs. We publish annual financial reports for transparency.

Can I sponsor a specific project or community?

Yes, we offer targeted giving options where you can support specific projects, communities, or program areas. Contact our donations team for more information.
