About Ashmay Charity

Learn about our mission, our team, and the principles that guide our work to create lasting change in communities worldwide.

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by a group of passionate volunteers, Ashmay Charity began as a small community initiative in response to a devastating natural disaster. What started as emergency relief quickly evolved into a comprehensive approach to sustainable community development.

Over the past 14 years, we've grown from a local response team to an international organization with programs in 15 countries, but our core commitment remains the same: empowering communities to build their own pathways out of poverty.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To empower underserved communities through sustainable development initiatives in education, healthcare, and economic opportunity, fostering hope and creating lasting change.

Vision

A world where every person has the opportunity to thrive, with access to quality education, healthcare, and the resources needed to build a sustainable future.

Values

  • Community-led development
  • Transparency and accountability
  • Cultural sensitivity
  • Sustainable solutions
  • Collaborative partnerships

Our Approach

Our methodology is built on principles that ensure sustainable, community-led development.

Community-Led

We work with local communities to identify needs and co-design solutions that are culturally appropriate and sustainable.

Evidence-Based

Our programs are designed based on research and continuously evaluated to ensure effectiveness and impact.

Sustainable

We focus on building local capacity and creating systems that continue to benefit communities long after our direct involvement ends.
