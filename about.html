About Ashmay Charity

Learn about our mission, our team, and the principles that guide our work to create lasting change in communities worldwide.

OUR JOURNEY

Building Hope,
Transforming Lives

Founded in 2010 by passionate volunteers, Ashmay Charity began as a small community initiative responding to a devastating natural disaster. What started as emergency relief quickly evolved into a comprehensive approach to sustainable development.

Over 14 years, we've grown from a local response team to an international organization with programs in 15 countries, maintaining our core commitment: empowering communities to build their own pathways out of poverty.
