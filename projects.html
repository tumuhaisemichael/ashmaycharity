Rural School Construction

Active Project

Active

Project Overview

Building schools in remote villages to provide education access to children who currently have no formal schooling opportunities. This initiative focuses on constructing sustainable, safe learning environments with proper facilities.

Objectives

  • Construct 5 school buildings in remote villages
  • Provide education access to 500+ children
  • Train 20 local teachers
  • Establish sustainable maintenance programs

Timeline

Project started in January 2024 with an estimated completion date of December 2024. Currently in the construction phase with 3 of 5 schools operational.

Partners

This project is implemented in partnership with local education departments, community leaders, and international education foundations.
